# SEO Content Plan — Bussey & Bussey

Five article targets, grounded in real keyword data (SEO Scout: volume, competition
index, CPC, word-count-to-win). All EASY difficulty / winnable. Focused on what Bussey
actually does — process & operations automation for service businesses — not generic AI.

Total addressable volume: ~9,380 searches/month across the five.

## Cluster structure
- **Standalone pillars:** #1 (automate customer service), #4 (workflow automation tools).
- **BPA cluster:** #2, #3, #5 all target "business process automation" variants — write
  these to interlink and build topical authority around process automation.

## Writing approach (per earlier decisions)
- Word-count targets are guides, not gospel (Scout's formula = beat longest competitor +10%).
  The real win condition: be the most complete, genuinely useful result for the query.
  Don't pad to hit a number — completeness drives length.
- Content substance comes from the founder (real expertise = the E-E-A-T edge competitors
  can't fake). Claude shapes/structures into the publishable article. This keeps it grounded
  and avoids the "all-AI-written" penalty.
- Recommended sequence by effort-to-payoff: #1 first (fast win, ~590 words), then #5 and #2/#3
  (mid), then #4 (the big flagship) once the domain has some authority.
- All are EASY by keyword difficulty, but a new domain still takes weeks–months to rank.
  This is the map; the climb is real.
